ABOUT ME

I am a Software Engineer with a passion for game design. I am currently a Computer Science and Game Design major at the University of California, Santa Cruz, expected to graduate in June 2018.

I value building projects that excite users, whether that's a much-anticipated game or a development tool designed to help others create more efficiently. I love working with both engineers and designers, and have developed tools to allow design and engineering to mix fluidly. Nothing is more rewarding to me than watching a concept flow smoothly across teams, with everyone building passion for the project as it comes together.
