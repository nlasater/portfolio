MY STORY

"It's a dangerous business, Frodo, going out your door. You step onto the road, and if you don't keep your feet, there's no knowing where you might be swept off to."

~ J.R.R. Tolkien

A Foundation in Creativity

  • Art was always my thing. I wanted to create my own world and fill it with the beauty I saw around me.
  • I never had the patience to shop and buy the things I wanted - if I had a vision, I'd try to create it first. Whether I'd tried something similar before or not. I ended up with lots of disasters and a few injuries, but I always loved it!

Discovering a Love of the Technical

  • I was fascinated by science and logic. It wasn't separate frommy creativity - it was just a more ordered form of it.
  • When I discovered programming, I fell in love. It's detail oriented, creative, and just plain fun.
  • I discovered a passion for clever solutions - it's like art, but there's a winner!

Passion for Play

  • I have always wanted to create things that excite people, from my middle-school paintings to my most complex projects.
  • Play is a uniquely free space, a place to explore and experiement without fear.
  • I love to create games because they bring delight, without losing meaning or falling flat. They're powerful and simple, and I'm just passionate about them!
